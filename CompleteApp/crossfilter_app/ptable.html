Welcome to Collaborative Data Analysis for DFT Benchmarking

Here is a collaborative web platform hosted by the Materials Science and Engineering Division at NIST for the Materials Genome Initiative to benchmark DFT calculations in VASP and DMol3, from where you can obtain material properties at infinite k-points density and use a Pade extrapolation fit to interactively benchmark your data. Hover over elements in red in the periodic table to view the Volume per atom (A^3), Bulk Modulus (GPa) and its pressure derivative estimated at an infinite k-points density by the Pade extrapolation, each in their fcc, bcc and hcp phases.
