Welcome to Collaborative Data Analysis for DFT Benchmarking

Here is a collaborative web platform hosted by the Materials Science and Engineering Division at NIST for the Materials Genome Initiative to benchmark DFT calculations in VASP and DMol3, from where you can obtain material properties at infinite k-points density and view the Pade extrapolation fit to benchmark data.

  • Select the Code, Exchange Correlation and Structure of Interest from drop down menus
  • Hover over elements in red in the periodic table to view the Volume per atom (A^3), Bulk Modulus (GPa) and its pressure derivative estimated at an infinite k-points density by the Pade extrapolation
  • Select the element of interest from the checkboxes below the periodic table
  • Select the property of interest
  • Choose x- and y- values
  • Click on Crossfilter and Plot to plot your selection of data
  • Click on PadeAnalysis to visualize the performance of Pade extrapolation on selected data
